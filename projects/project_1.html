FreeBee

FreeBee is a product concept for a mobile app that builds networks through a marketplace of free and local things. Need a bike to get to school? Want to get rid of your old desk? FreeBee knows someone who wants to help you.

How does FreeBee work?

Join and instantly get dialed in to the most convenient network of free stuff in your local area. Snap a pic of a free item you'd like to offer up to a neighbor, or search the listings for the great stuff your neighbors are posting.

Every time you give something away, earn a honeycomb as a badge of appreciation. Your honeycomb score is public, so everyone knows how awesome you are!
